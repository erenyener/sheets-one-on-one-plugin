## Setup

1- For the first setup Click 1-1 Helper from the top menu and click Setup.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/LLXTH2b.png" border="3" width="600" >

2- From the modal, enter your cycle time for 1-1s. 

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/5ge8Iv9.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/P10AVfa.png" border="3" width="600" >

3- Add your custom Fields. Those fields are not required, but, they are good for filtering people list.

For example, if you want to filter employes who were entered before 12 months, you can simple add Employment Start Date as a custom field.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/9DHnzXD.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/Ju4VQjo.png" border="3" width="600" >

4- Click to download csv.txt file and fill out people data.


<img src="https://i.imgur.com/NRP0Ooa.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/BMljPMf.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/22lpClW.png" border="3" width="600" >

Click Save and exit once finish your data.


5- Upload csv.txt 

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/dgKMgWC.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/QMyCmoq.png" border="3" width="600" >
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/8kTRtGa.png" border="3" width="600" >

Once upload is finished, you can click to complete setup.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/9GzdqJ8.png" border="3"  width="600" >

Setup has completed, you can close the modal.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/Yg8VyK4.png" border="3" width="600" >

Finally, you should see 1-1 sheet with your custom fields and default fields.
